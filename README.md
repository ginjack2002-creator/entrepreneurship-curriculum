# Youth Entrepreneurship Curriculum

Collection of entrepreneurship education resources, business startup guides, and financial literacy materials for K-12 and beyond. This repository supports Discovery Collective's mission of empowering students with real-world business knowledge and entrepreneurial thinking skills.

## Folder Structure

| Folder | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `curriculum-research/` | Academic research on entrepreneurship education, pedagogy, and best practices |
| `business-guides/` | Setup guides, marketing guides, workbooks, and step-by-step business resources |
| `financial-literacy/` | Cashflow management, financing options, business fundamentals, and money skills |
| `sales-marketing/` | Funnels, marketing strategy, sales mastery, and customer acquisition |
| `youth-entrepreneurship/` | K-12 specific resources: Kidpreneurs, student business ideas, and age-appropriate ventures |
| `analysis/` | Document analysis summaries and resource reviews |
